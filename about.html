Our Stories

"Every thread has a history. Every garment has a soul"

ThriftLab wasn't born in a boardroom, it started with a simple idea, that style shouldn't cost the earth. We began as a small passion project, hunting for hidden gems in dusty warehouses and forgotten closets. What started as a quest for unique vintage pieces quickly evolved into a movement.

Our Milestones

  • 2021: The Spark

    Launched ThriftLab from a small garage with just 50 hand-picked items. Our first drop sold out in 24 hours, proving that sustainable street style was more than just a trend.

  • 2022: The Streetwear Collection

    Defined our identity. We moved beyond simple thrifting to curation, launching our signature streetwear edit that focused on 90s and Y2K urban aesthetics.

  • 2023: Global Reach & Collaboration

    Partnered with SVG brand for an exclusive upcycled capsule collection. Expanded our shipping worldwide, bringing the ThriftLab culture to over 15 countries.

Our Values

  1. Authenticity: We don't do fakes. Every piece is inspected, verified, and curated. We celebrate the raw, unfiltered energy of streetwear culture.
  2. Sustainability: Fashion shouldn't be disposable. By choosing ThriftLab, you are directly reducing textile waste and participating in a circular economy. Look good, do good.
  3. Community: We are more than a store, we are a tribe. From the skaters to the artists, the students to the creators. ThriftLab is a platform for everyone who uses style to speak without saying a word.
